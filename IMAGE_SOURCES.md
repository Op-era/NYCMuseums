## How to Download Images

### Option 1: Wikimedia Commons (Free, Public Domain or Creative Commons)
1. Go to https://commons.wikimedia.org/
2. Search for the museum name
3. Download high-resolution images
4. Check license (most are free to use)

### Option 2: Official Museum Websites
1. Visit the museum's official website
2. Look for "Press" or "Media" section
3. Download press-approved images
4. Follow attribution requirements if any

### Option 3: Unsplash/Pexels (Free Stock Photos)
1. Go to https://unsplash.com/ or https://www.pexels.com/
2. Search for museum names
3. Download free-to-use images
4. No attribution required (but appreciated)

## Image Specifications
- **Recommended size**: 800x600 pixels or larger
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Aspect ratio**: 4:3 or 16:9 works best
- **File naming**: Use the exact names in the code:
  - broadway-museum.jpg
  - moving-image.jpg
  - met-museum.jpg
  - natural-history.jpg
  - moma.jpg
  - lincoln-center.jpg
  - intrepid.jpg
  - 911-memorial.jpg
  - frick.jpg
  - hall-of-science.jpg
  - placeholder.jpg (fallback image)
